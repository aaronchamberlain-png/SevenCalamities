In-Game Dates: Ches 1

Players complete characters and their back stories which are integrated into the world, and learn about the world and recent events, including the Impilturian Earthquake on the 17th of Hammer, 1502 DR.

In-Game Dates: Ches 1 - 2

Ellawain, Ikari, and Delon attend the king's ball where they learn of rumors, uncover secrets, and compete in challenges. The king tasks the characters to to accompany the prince to West-Over-Farthering, where they will meet up with additional party members. On the journey, the party are attacked by brigands at the sword bridge and are assisted by Seraphina Vale in apprehending two of them. The heroes continue to the crossroads where they meet Captain Wardstone Thane, Nhalos the Moondog, and defeat a duo of Ankheg.

In-Game Dates: Ches 3 - 4

Prince Edric, Ellawain ,Ikari, and Delon reach West-Over-Farthing and go to the Broken Keel Inn/Tavern to meet up with an expected companion, Vichenzo Visconti, who had arrived the night before by magical means. They are also unexpectedly joined by the bard, Stiora Autumnblossom who agrees to travel with them and chronicle their "legendary exploits". After averting a possible problem between the prince and the barkeep, the heroes set forth.

Together, the companions reach the outskirts of West-Over-Farthing and are accosted by a band of marauding goblins at the Western Watchtower. Victorious, the adventurers head north along the chasm to locate the point at which Narmus Sting had fallen. They locate the position, and descend to the depths of the chasm on ropes, fighting off drooling climbers on the way. They locate Narmus Sting's body and an entrace to a mysterious underground chamber of worked stone.
