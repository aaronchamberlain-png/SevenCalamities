Quest Log

Travel to West-Over-Farthing

At the request of the Crown, the adventurers are dispatched to the village of West-Over-Farthing, where troubling reports have emerged in the aftermath of the great earthquake. There they will be joined by Vichenzo Visconti.

Status: In Progress

Find Narmus Sting's Body

At the request of the Crown, the adventurers are tasked with locating the remains of Narmus Sting, the famed ranger and former companion of King Alric, who is believed to have perished when the great earthquake opened a massive chasm near West-Over-Farthing. Though many assume he fell to his death, no body has ever been recovered. The king hopes to grant his old friend a proper burial, and quell whispers.

Status: Incomplete

Investigate the Earthquake

At the request of the Crown, the adventurers are dispatched to survey the region surrounding West-Over-Farthing and the effects of the great earthquake. Reports speak of lingering tremors that continue to reshape the landscape. The findings may help determine whether the disaster was merely a natural catastrophe—or the first sign of something far more troubling beneath Impiltur.

Status: Incomplete

The King's Ball

At the personal request of the crown, the adventurers are summoned to attend a lavish royal ball within the ancient halls of the Citadel of the Stalwart King, where nobles, commanders, priests, and influential figures from across Impiltur will gather. No explanation accompanies the invitation.

Status: Completed
