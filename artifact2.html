Ledger of Perfect Fortune

Wondrous item, Legendary (requires attunement)

Description

The Ledger of Perfect Fortune is an endless enchanted scroll housed within a gilded spindle of blackened brass, its parchment forever unrolling as invisible quills inscribe every triumph, tragedy, whisper, and accident occurring within two miles of its presence. Created to shield rulers, healers, magistrates, and other “essential” figures from harm, the ledger twists fate itself, siphoning luck from the powerless and concentrating fortune upon those it deems valuable. A merchant survives a collapse because a stranger is crushed beneath falling stone; a councilor escapes assassination because a fire erupts in a crowded tenement instead. The scroll glows with shifting lines of emerald and crimson ink that braid together like veins, visibly tracing where fortune is granted and where catastrophe is redirected. Though revered as a tool of order and prosperity, the ledger inevitably rots the society around it, as entire districts become sacrificial reservoirs of misfortune, proving the fatal flaw at its core: the arrogant belief that luck, suffering, and human worth can ever be fairly managed.

History

The Ledger of Perfect Fortune first came into the possession of an unknown servant of Beshaba. The Maid of Misfortune imparted an impossible gift: an enchanted scroll that endlessly inscribed every death, accident, betrayal, and triumph occurring within the sphere of its presence. Beshaba whispered that suffering could be managed, redirected, and sacrificed so that “worthy” lives might continue unburdened by fate. The unknown servant sought a successor to pass the vile gift to. That successor was Lord Blance Vramor, the infamous grain merchant of Lyrabar, whose greed and indifference made him the perfect master for the ledger’s cruel philosophy. Presented as a divine instrument capable of safeguarding commerce and preserving societal order, the scroll quickly became the hidden engine behind the disasters that plagued the poorer districts of Impiltur, feeding Vramor’s prosperity through orchestrated waves of engineered misfortune.
