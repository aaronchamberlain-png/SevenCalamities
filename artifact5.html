Rod of the Ascendent Evoker

Legendary | Rod | Very Rare (requires attunement by a Sorcerer or Wizard)

Powers and Abilities

  • Echoing Evocation

    Once per long rest, immediately after you cast an Evocation spell of 1st level or higher using a spell slot, the rod stores the magical pattern of that spell.

    At the beginning of your next turn, you may cast the same spell again without expending a spell slot. The magical energy is lost if not used that turn.

  • Ascendant Barrage Whenever you cast an Evocation spell of 1st level or higher, the rod releases bolts of force that seek your enemies.

  • After the spell is resolved, create a number of Magic Missiles equal to the spell's level. These darts function exactly as those created by the Magic Missile spell, except:

    Every dart must target a creature that is either:
o	a target of the evocation spell, or 
o	within the spell's area of effect. 
o	The darts are considered part of the spell's resolution 
        and require no action. 
o	The darts automatically hit, each dealing 1d4 + 1 
        force damage.

History

Forged from silvery mithral entwined around a core of crimson crystal, the Rod of the Ascendant Evoker hums softly with restrained arcane power. Brilliant veins of ruby light pulse beneath its polished surface, growing brighter whenever destructive magic is invoked. Legends claim the rod was crafted by an archmage who sought to perfect the art of overwhelming magical offense—not by casting more powerful spells, but by ensuring that every spell cast echoed with lingering destructive force. Those who wield the rod often find stray sparks dancing along its length, while the air around it crackles with barely contained magical energy.

"Power squandered is power denied. — Quote by Ultheera Windmere, the original owner of the Rod of the Ascendant Evoker
